Splitr is a smart pace calculator for runners, cyclists, and anyone who thinks in splits.

Enter any two values — speed, pace, distance, or time — and Splitr instantly calculates everything else. No tapping a button. No switching screens. Just the numbers you need, the moment you need them.

**How it works**
Type a distance and a time, and Splitr shows you your pace and speed. Type a pace and a distance, and it tells you your finish time. Change any field and everything updates live.

**What you can calculate**
- Speed in mph and km/h
- Pace per mile and pace per kilometre
- Distance in miles and kilometres
- Total time in hours, minutes, and seconds

**Distance shortcuts**
Tap 800m, 1.5K, 3K, 5K, 10K, Half Marathon, or Marathon to instantly set the distance — then just enter a pace or time to get everything else.

**Built for clarity**
A clean, dark interface keeps the focus on your numbers. Fields you've entered are highlighted in blue. Derived values are calculated automatically and shown in a distinct style so you always know what you typed vs. what was worked out.

**Save your presets**
Save any calculation with a custom name. Restore it with one tap. Reorder and rename your presets whenever you like. Your presets sync automatically across your devices via iCloud.

**Home screen widget**
Add the Splitr widget to your home screen to see your most recently saved preset at a glance — speed, pace, distance, and time — without opening the app.

**Share results**
Share any calculation via text, email, or any app on your phone.

**100% free. 100% private.**
Splitr is free and always will be. No subscription. No sign-up. No ads. No tracking. Your data never leaves your device. There's an optional tip jar if you'd like to support development — but every feature is free, no strings attached.

Whether you're planning a marathon, checking your tempo pace, or converting a training plan from miles to kilometres, Splitr gives you the answer before you've finished typing.
