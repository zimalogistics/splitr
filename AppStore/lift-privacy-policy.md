# Privacy Policy for Lift

_Last updated: March 2026_

## Overview

Lift is a personal workout tracking app. We take your privacy seriously — the app is designed to work without an account and keeps your data under your control.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Lift does not require you to create an account, and we have no servers that receive or store your workout data.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All workout data you log — including exercises, sets, reps, weights, and notes — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

## iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud on your device and have iCloud Drive enabled, your workout data may be synced to your private iCloud account via CloudKit. This sync is:

- **Private**: only accessible by you, through your personal Apple ID
- **Encrypted**: Apple encrypts iCloud data in transit and at rest
- **Controlled by you**: you can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings → Apple ID → iCloud

We never have access to your iCloud data.

## Exercise Catalogue

The built-in exercise catalogue (800+ exercises) is sourced from the open-source [free-exercise-db](https://github.com/yuhonas/free-exercise-db) project on GitHub. This data is downloaded over HTTPS and cached locally on your device. No personal information is transmitted during this process.

## Notifications

Lift uses local notifications for the rest timer feature. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and are never sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings → Notifications → Lift.

## No Advertising or Tracking

- We do not show advertisements
- We do not use analytics SDKs or tracking tools
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties
- We do not use cookies or web tracking

## Children's Privacy

Lift does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
