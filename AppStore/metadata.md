## Description (max 4000 chars)

Splitr is a smart pace calculator for runners, cyclists, and anyone who thinks in splits.

Enter any two values — speed, pace, distance, or time — and Splitr instantly calculates everything else. No tapping a button. No switching screens. Just the numbers you need.

**How it works**
Type a distance and a time, and Splitr shows you your pace and speed. Type a pace and a distance, and it tells you your finish time. Change any field and everything updates live.

**What you can calculate**
- Speed in mph and km/h
- Pace per mile and pace per kilometre
- Distance in miles and kilometres
- Total time in hours, minutes, and seconds

**Built for clarity**
A clean, dark interface keeps the focus on your numbers. User-entered values are highlighted in blue. Derived values are calculated automatically and shown in a distinct style so you always know what you typed vs. what was worked out.

**Distance shortcuts**
Tap 5K, 10K, Half Marathon, or Marathon to instantly set the distance — then just enter a pace or time to get everything else.

**Save your presets**
Save any calculation with a custom name. Restore it with one tap. Reorder and rename your presets whenever you like. Your presets sync automatically across your devices via iCloud.

**Home screen widget**
Add the Splitr widget to your home screen to see your most recently saved preset at a glance — no need to open the app.

**Share results**
Share any calculation or saved entry via text, email, or any app on your phone.

Whether you're planning a marathon, checking your tempo pace, or converting a training plan from miles to kilometres, Splitr gives you the answer before you've finished typing.

## Hosting the Privacy Policy (required before submission)

Apple requires a publicly accessible privacy policy URL. Easiest option:

1. Go to your GitHub repo → Settings → Pages
2. Set source to "Deploy from a branch" → branch: main, folder: /AppStore
3. Your privacy policy will be at: https://zimalogistics.github.io/converter/privacy-policy.html
4. Paste that URL into App Store Connect under App Information → Privacy Policy URL
