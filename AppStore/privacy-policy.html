Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Splitr ("the app") is a pace and speed calculator for runners and athletes. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data We Collect

None. Splitr does not collect, transmit to us, or share any personal information. We have no servers and receive no data from your use of the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your calculation presets locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults). This data is deleted when you remove the app.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud on your device, Splitr uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to sync your saved presets across your own devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Splitr has no access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Splitr does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Network Access

Splitr makes no network requests of its own. Any network activity is solely Apple's iCloud sync infrastructure, if enabled on your device.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
