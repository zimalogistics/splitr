Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the calculator?

Enter any two values (speed, pace, distance, or time) and Splitr instantly calculates the rest. No button needed. Tap a distance shortcut (5K, 10K, Half Marathon, etc.) to fill the distance field automatically.

How do I save a calculation?

Tap the bookmark icon to save your current values as a named preset. Your presets sync across all your iPhone devices via iCloud.

How do I share my results?

Tap the share icon to copy or share a formatted summary of your current calculation.

What does the widget show?

The home screen widget displays your most recently saved preset with pace, speed, distance, and time. It updates automatically when you save a new preset.

Is the app really free?

Every feature in Splitr is available at no cost. The optional Tip Jar in Settings lets you support development if you choose.

Does Splitr collect my data?

No. Splitr has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. Your presets are stored locally and in your personal iCloud account. See our Privacy Policy for details.
