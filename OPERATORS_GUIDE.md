## Calculator

Splitr converts between speed, pace, distance, and time in real time. Enter any two values and everything else calculates automatically — no submit button needed.

**Fields:**
- **Speed** — mph (left) and km/h (right)
- **Pace** — min/mile (left) and min/km (right)
- **Distance** — miles (left) and kilometres (right)
- **Time** — HH:MM:SS

**How it works:**
1. Tap any field and type a value
2. Tap a second field in a different section and type a value
3. All remaining fields fill in immediately
4. Blue fields = values you typed. Teal/grey fields = values Splitr calculated.

**Clearing fields:**
- Tap the **×** button on any field to clear just that value
- Tap **Clear All** to reset everything

**Input formats accepted:**
- Time: `8:30:00`, `8:30`, `830` (= 8:30), `14230` (= 1:42:30)
- Pace: `8:30`, `830` (= 8:30 min/mile)
- Distance: decimals or whole numbers

## Distance Shortcuts

A row of quick-tap buttons appears below the Distance section:

`800m` · `1.5K` · `3K` · `5K` · `10K` · `Half` · `Marathon`

Tap any shortcut to instantly set the distance. Then enter a pace or time to get the full calculation.

## Saved Presets

**Saving a calculation:**
1. Enter at least one value
2. Tap **Save** (appears when any field has a value)
3. Give the preset a name (or tap Skip to save without one)

**Using a preset:**
- Tap any saved preset card to restore all its values into the calculator

**Managing presets:**
- **Rename:** Long-press a preset → Rename
- **Reorder:** Long-press a preset → Move, then drag to new position
- **Delete:** Swipe left on a preset, or long-press → Delete

**iCloud sync:** Presets automatically sync across all your iPhone devices signed into the same Apple ID.

## Home Screen Widget

Splitr offers two widget sizes — **small** and **medium** — both showing your most recently saved preset.

**To add the widget:**
1. Long-press the home screen
2. Tap **+** in the top-left
3. Search for "Splitr"
4. Choose small or medium
5. Tap **Add Widget**

The widget updates automatically when you save a new preset.

## Share

Tap the **↑ share** icon in the top toolbar (visible when any field has a value) to share a plain-text summary of the current calculation via Messages, Mail, or any other app.

## Tip Jar

Tap the **☕** button in the top-right corner to leave an optional tip. Three amounts available: $0.99, $2.99, $4.99. Tips are one-time purchases and support continued development.

---

## Privacy

Splitr collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no sign-up. Your data never leaves your device (presets are stored locally and in your personal iCloud account — never on our servers).
